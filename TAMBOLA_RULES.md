## 3. GAME FLOW

### Setup Phase:
1. Admin creates game with date, time, and prize structure
2. System generates 600 tickets (100 Full Sheets)
3. Players browse and select tickets
4. Players book tickets via WhatsApp (payment offline)
5. Admin confirms bookings after WhatsApp payment verification

### Live Game Phase:
1. Game starts automatically at scheduled time
2. Voice caller announces numbers with traditional call names:
   - "Number 22 - Two Little Ducks"
   - "Number 88 - Two Fat Ladies"
   - "Number 11 - Legs Eleven"
   - etc.
3. Numbers called every 10 seconds automatically
4. Players mark numbers on their tickets in real-time
5. Winners claim prizes as patterns complete
6. Game continues until Full House is declared

### Claiming Prizes:
- Players must mark numbers in real-time during live game
- Auto-marking happens when number is called
- Multiple winners can claim line prizes
- First/Second House are exclusive (one winner each)
- Full House can have multiple winners if simultaneous
